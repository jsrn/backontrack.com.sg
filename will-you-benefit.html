Deciding whether you will benefit from
Life Coaching.
Score each of the following questions from from 1 to 10, with 10 being the highest:
  1. I am ready to seek for more work-life balance
  2. I am ready to improve my relationships
  3. I am ready to find and live my life’s calling and gifting
  4. I am ready and eager to deal with my self-limiting beliefs and behaviour
  5. I am ready to find a new way to achieve a sense of fulfilment
  6. I am ready to reduce the stress in my life
  7. I am ready to decide on a new career development strategy
  8. I am ready to change my habits
  9. I am ready to change my current approach to setting my plans and actions so that I can achieve my goals
  10. I want to be the best version of myself
If the sum total is above 40, you will likely benefit from Life Coaching.
