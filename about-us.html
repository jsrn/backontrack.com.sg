About.

Gareth has over 20 years experience in coaching and counselling people from different cultures, as well as ethnic, social and technical backgrounds. He has a Masters in Pastoral Counselling. Based in Singapore, he has travelled extensively throughout Asia, teaching and providing troubleshooting support to businesses and individuals.
